Specialised Orthopaedic Procedures

Our single-specialty focus allows us to provide unparalleled depth of expertise in a range of elective orthopaedic surgeries.

Total hip replacement (arthroplasty) is a highly successful procedure to relieve pain and restore function for patients with advanced hip arthritis. We connect you with surgeons specializing in the latest minimally-invasive techniques for faster recovery.

For those suffering from severe knee pain due to osteoarthritis or injury, knee replacement can be life-changing. Our partner hospitals utilize advanced robotic-assisted technology for precision and optimal outcomes.

We facilitate a range of spine procedures, including discectomy and spinal fusion, performed by highly experienced neurosurgeons and orthopaedic spine specialists to address conditions like herniated discs and spinal stenosis.

From rotator cuff repair and shoulder replacement to ACL reconstruction, we connect active individuals with top surgeons to help them return to the activities they love.
