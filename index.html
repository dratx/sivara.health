Your Questions, Answered

Understanding the quality, value, and process of receiving care in Thailand is key to making a confident decision. Here are answers to some common questions.

The quality of care in Thailand's leading private hospitals is world-class and meets the most rigorous international standards. We ensure this by focusing on:
  • JCI Accreditation: We exclusively partner with hospitals accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), which is recognised globally as the gold standard for patient safety and quality of care.
  • Leading Hospitals: Our partner facilities are consistently ranked among the world's best and most visited private hospitals, treating patients from every corner of the globe.
  • Expert Specialists: Surgeons in these hospitals are often internationally trained and are leaders in their fields, utilising cutting-edge technology to achieve outstanding clinical outcomes.
The excellent value is a direct result of Thailand's economic structure, not a compromise on quality. The significant cost savings are due to lower operational and infrastructure overheads compared to countries like the UK or US. This efficiency allows our partner hospitals to invest in state-of-the-art technology and world-class facilities while offering procedures at a more accessible price point. You receive care of the highest international calibre without the exorbitant cost.
Choosing Thailand with Sivara Health offers a unique combination of benefits that are difficult to find elsewhere. You gain:
  • World-Class Quality: Access to JCI-accredited hospitals and elite surgeons who deliver exceptional medical outcomes.
  • Exceptional Value: Receive the highest standard of care while benefiting from significant cost savings, allowing you to focus on your recovery without financial strain.
  • Immediate Access: Avoid long waiting lists common in other countries and get the timely treatment you need to restore your quality of life.
  • A Restorative Environment: Recuperate in a country renowned for its warm hospitality, providing a serene and comfortable setting for your recovery period.
Your safety and confidence are our highest priorities. Unlike a booking platform, Sivara Health is physician-led. Our UK-trained physician co-founder personally reviews your case and vets every specialist we partner with. We act as your dedicated advocate, ensuring you are matched with the right expert at a world-class, accredited facility, and feel supported and informed throughout your entire journey.
Your care continues long after your procedure. We bridge the distance by facilitating clear post-operative communication and follow-up consultations with your surgeon in Thailand. We also provide you with all necessary medical documentation and guidance for liaising with your local doctor or physiotherapist back home, ensuring a smooth, supported, and seamless recovery.
While booking directly is an option, our package price includes a comprehensive, physician-led concierge service designed to ensure your safety and a seamless journey. This covers your initial virtual triage with our UK doctor, vetting of surgeons, end-to-end travel and logistics management, 24/7 in-country support, and lifelong aftercare coordination. Our modest fee for this premium service is included in your transparent quote, which still represents a significant saving of 50-70% compared to typical costs in the UK or US. You get a fully managed, expertly guided experience without sacrificing value.
